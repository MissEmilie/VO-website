More About Emilie

Heya! I'm Emilie, a Canadian born Indigenous, Irish and Croatian voice actress. Some of my earliest memories are of playing Super Mario World and Theme Park with my dad and uncle so it's no wonder I love voice acting for video games so much. That's not to say I don't love my animations too! I only started voice acting because of how much I loved JoJo's Bizarre Adventure (it's my life's biggest goal to one day voice in JoJo!).

I'm currently studying at McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario for my bachelor's degree in Earth and Environmental Sciences with a minor in Computer Science. I actually coded and designed this entire website myself with a good chunk of the skills I've gotten out of my degree!

When I'm not acting, I love playing video games (my top three right now are Minecraft, Genshin Impact and Fallout: New Vegas), playing some DnD, listening or dancing to bad music, reading tarot, drinking iced lattes or oil painting.

I am also on the autism spectrum and a member of the LGBTQA+ community as bisexual, asexual and non-binary.
